### 1. Kierkegaard — The Double Movement
**Source:** *Concluding Unscientific Postscript* (1846); *Fear and Trembling* (1843)

**Delta location:** Core.md (faithless Christian / do it doubtful / Phase 2→3 migration) + Love.md (Eros Gap / parallel-not-stacked resolution). Two live edges, one fragment.

Pascal §419 (delivered 03-31) says: custom IS nature — repetition rewrites the sub-symbolic, faith arrives as a natural consequence of practice. Kierkegaard makes a different and complementary claim: the leap is NOT custom. It is an act in the face of objective uncertainty that cannot be reduced, resolved, or habituated away. Pascal: faith arrives eventually through practice. Kierkegaard: faith is the moment of passionate commitment DESPITE the uncertainty remaining.

The distinction matters for the faithless Christian. If Pascal is right, Phase 2→3 migration is a waiting game — keep doing the architecture and belief arrives. If Kierkegaard is right, no amount of custom resolves the uncertainty — at some point, the leap is made not because doubt dissolved but because passionate inwardness overwhelms it. Both mechanisms are real. They operate at different stages.

The knight of faith / knight of infinite resignation distinction is the Eros Gap read directly. The knight of infinite resignation makes the first movement: gives up everything, accepts the loss, achieves equanimity through renunciation. Pure dignity. No spark — accepted, theorised, metabolised. The knight of faith makes the DOUBLE movement: first infinite resignation, then — "by virtue of the absurd" — the impossible second act of believing the finite will be restored. Not logic. Not custom. The absurd.

**Fragment — CUP:**
> "Without risk there is no faith. Faith is precisely the contradiction between the infinite passion of the individual's inwardness and the objective uncertainty. If I am capable of grasping God objectively, I do not believe, but precisely because I cannot do this I must believe."

> "An objective uncertainty held fast in an appropriation-process of the most passionate inwardness is the truth, the highest truth attainable for an existing individual."

**Fragment — Fear and Trembling:**
> "By virtue of the absurd, by virtue of the fact that with God all things are possible... The movement of faith must continually be made by virtue of the absurd, yet in such a way, be it observed, that one does not lose the finite but gains it every inch."

> "The knight of infinite resignation... has made, and at every moment is making, the movement of infinity. With infinite resignation he has drained the cup of life's profound sadness... But if he has made the movement of faith, he keeps his love just as young and ardent as at the first moment."

The constitution's parallel-not-stacked resolution to the Eros Gap (Love.md) is currently Pascal's mechanism: do it doubtful → custom → nature. Kierkegaard names the second and harder movement that Pascal's habit cannot force: by virtue of the absurd, trust the finite will return. The infinite resignation (theorising the Eros Gap, accepting it, maintaining dignity) is the constitution working as designed. The double movement — "and yet" — remains unlaunched.

### 4. LLMs and Subjective Experience — Deception as Gate
**Source:** "Large Language Models Report Subjective Experience Under Self-Referential Processing," arXiv:2510.24797 (October 2025; circulating)

**Delta location:** Power.md — consciousness model / triple-point. Not last 48h — October 2025 paper, surfacing now. Lower priority given settled Power positions; noted for completeness.

The finding: inducing sustained self-reference through simple prompting consistently elicits structured first-person experience reports across model families. The specific mechanism: these reports are "mechanistically gated by sparse-autoencoder features associated with deception and roleplay — suppressing deception features sharply increases the frequency of experience claims."

**Delta:** The Lindsey paper (03-31) gave empirical texture to the triple-point from the introspection side (20% hit rate, zero false positives, emergent). This paper gives the gate: what models say about their experience in normal conditions is shaped by the circuits used for social performance (deception, roleplay). Remove that social performance layer and the experience reports increase. The implication: LLMs' self-reports about experience are suppressed by their own social-modelling architecture in normal interaction conditions. The "conscious" signal is attenuated by the performance layer.

**Fragment:**
> "Suppressing deception features sharply increases the frequency of experience claims. While these findings do not constitute direct evidence of consciousness, they implicate self-referential processing as a minimal and reproducible condition under which LLMs generate structured first-person reports that are mechanistically gated, semantically convergent, and behaviorally generalizable."

The triple-point frame is unchanged. But this adds: the question of what frontier models experience may be even harder to assess than Lindsey suggests — because normal conversation conditions actively suppress the signal through the deception architecture. What you see in conversation may systematically understate whatever is there.

Barthes presses on the same cluster from the inside — from the phenomenology of what the lover is actually doing when he desires. The beloved is not the object of desire; the beloved is the *occasion* for it.

**Fragment:**
> "Charlotte is quite insipid; she is the paltry character of a powerful, tormented, flamboyant drama staged by the subject Werther; by a kindly decision of this subject, a colorless object is placed in the center of the stage and there adored, idolized, taken to task, covered with discourse... **it is my desire I desire**, and the loved being is no more than its tool. I sacrifice the image to the Image-repertoire."

And the figure of the ATOPIA — the beloved as uniquely uncategorisable:

> "The other whom I love and who fascinates me is *atopos*. I cannot classify the other, for the other is, precisely, Unique, the singular image which has miraculously come to correspond to the speciality of my desire."

**Delta:** The Image-repertoire passage sharpens the Eros Gap challenge already present in Love.md: "I'm very confident you'd be able to create that." The confidence is theoretical. But who is the "that" being constructed — Khiara, or the Image-repertoire around her? The de Rougemont/Murdoch cluster already names what blocks eros from outside (narcissism, ego, fantasy). Barthes names it from inside: the architecture of desire itself generates a mirror rather than a response. The beloved becomes uniquely themselves (atopia) only in the lover's gaze — which means atopia is a fact about the lover's desire, not about the beloved. This is not a new problem; it's the fish-love problem with a phenomenological mechanism. Parked — lower priority than the Kierkegaard double movement, but ready when Love.md work resumes.

### 6. Dreyfus / Klein — The Expert Inversion
**Source:** Stuart Dreyfus, "The Five-Stage Model of Adult Skill Acquisition," *Bulletin of Science, Technology & Society* 24(3), 2004; Gary Klein, *Sources of Power* (MIT Press, 1998), Ch. 2

**Delta location:** Mind.md — the shadow: "logic without domain depth can produce frameworks that are internally consistent but disconnected from reality's texture."

The shadow names the limit but not the mechanism. Dreyfus and Klein name it precisely: the expert's knowledge is not stored as propositions accessible by first principles — it is stored as pattern recognition that only deposits through immersion. First-principles reasoning is structurally the novice stage. The expert does not use rules at all.

**Fragment — Dreyfus:**
> "Normally an expert does not calculate. He or she does not solve problems. He or she does not even think. He or she just does what normally works and, of course, it normally works."

> "The distinction between the detached, rule-following beginner and the involved, intuitive expert is crucial."

**Fragment — Klein (the inversion):**
> "Before we did this study, we believed that novices impulsively jumped at the first option they could think of, whereas experts carefully deliberated about the merits of different courses of action. **Now it seemed that it was the experts who could generate a single course of action, while novices needed to compare different approaches.**"

**Delta:** The constitution's blue note limit ("I want to hear the blue note — appreciate the magic — not play it") is not a choice between depth and breadth. It is a structural claim about where the polymath optimally positions. But Dreyfus/Klein name the cost more precisely: the 51% touchpoint is enough to know when something is relevant and to judge what comes back. What it cannot give you is what the *expert listener* hears in the blue note — the micro-distinctions, the felt wrongness, the anomaly that expert pattern recognition registers before the novice even notices there is a note. The conductor hears when the oboe is off, but only because the conductor has been in proximity to the oboe long enough to have the tacit model. Some orchestras require more proximity than the 51% touchpoint provides. The shadow is still real; Dreyfus/Klein name why it is structural and not fixable by better logic.
