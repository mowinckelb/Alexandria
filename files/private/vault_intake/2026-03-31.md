### 3. Pascal — Custom Is Our Nature

**Delta location:** Core.md (faithless Christian / do it doubtful / Teddy Roosevelt pattern) + Power.md (faithless Christianity Phase 2→3 migration). The faithless Christian practising the covenant is not fighting his sub-symbolic ground truth — he IS rewriting it. Custom is not the substitute for nature; it is the form nature takes when shaped by habit. This resolves a subtle tension in the parallel-not-stacked framework: how does the symbolic layer become co-equal ground truth? Not through effort. Through repetition that becomes custom that becomes nature.

**Fragment:**
> "Custom is our nature. He who is accustomed to the faith believes in it, can no longer fear hell, and believes nothing else." (§419, Lafuma)

> "The heart has its reasons, which reason does not know. We feel it in a thousand things... It is the heart which experiences God, and not the reason. This, then, is faith; God felt by the heart, not by reason." (§277, Lafuma)

And the crucial passage from inside the Wager section (§233) — after the interlocutor objects that they cannot simply choose to believe:

> "Endeavour then to convince yourself, not by increase of proofs of God, but by the abatement of your passions. You would like to attain faith, and do not know the way... Follow the way by which they began; by acting as if they believed, taking the holy water, having masses said, &c. **Even this will naturally make you believe, and deaden your acuteness.**"

The arc: §233 (you must wager, reason can't settle it) → §277 (the heart grasps what reason cannot) → §419 (custom is what does the actual work). The wager is the intellectual argument for starting. Custom completes what reason begins and will cannot force. "Even this will naturally make you believe" — the word is *naturally*. Not effortfully. The mechanism is not voluntarist — custom rewrites nature.

**Delta sharpening:** "Do it doubtful" (Mind.md) and "the Teddy Roosevelt pattern" (Core.md) already hold the destination. Pascal removes the voluntarist misreading. This is not the symbolic layer overriding the sub-symbolic through effort. It is custom rewriting nature — the symbolic layer becoming sub-symbolic through repetition. Marcus Aurelius's prokheiron (03-30) and Pascal's §419 are the same insight from Roman Stoicism and French Catholicism. "Begin the morning" = "acting as if they believed." Both name repetition as the only mechanism that gets you from doubt to faith, from faithless to faithful, from the phase transition to the other side. Already happening.

### 4. Adam Smith — Sympathy as Substitution, and the Impartial Spectator (parked twice, now delivered)
**Source:** Smith, *The Theory of Moral Sentiments* (1759), Part I, Section I; Part III

**Delta location:** Mind.md (sympathy as epistemology / parallel-not-stacked / the filing system) + Love.md + Core.md (SF monk mode / solitude warning). Three distinct deltas in one source.

**Fragment — the substitution mechanism:**
> "As we have no immediate experience of what other men feel, we can form no idea of the manner in which they are affected, but by conceiving what we ourselves should feel in the like situation. Though our brother is upon the rack, as long as we ourselves are at our ease, our senses will never inform us of what he suffers. They never did, and never can, carry us beyond our own person, and it is by the imagination only that we can form any conception of what are his sensations."

> "**It is the impressions of our own senses only, not those of his, which our imaginations copy.** By the imagination we place ourselves in his situation, we conceive ourselves enduring all the same torments, we enter as it were into his body, and become in some measure the same person with him, and thence form some idea of his sensations, and even feel something which, though weaker in degree, is not altogether unlike them."

**Fragment — the impartial spectator:**
> "It is reason, principle, conscience, the inhabitant of the breast, the man within, the great judge and arbiter of our conduct. It is he who, whenever we are about to act so as to affect the happiness of others, calls to us, with a voice capable of astonishing the most presumptuous of our passions, that we are but one of the multitude, in no respect better than any other in it."

**Fragment — the parallel-not-stacked struggle:**

**Fragment — the social calibration requirement:**

### 6. Jack Lindsey / Anthropic — Emergent Introspective Awareness (January 2026, still circulating)
**Source:** Lindsey, "Emergent Introspective Awareness in Large Language Models," Anthropic research + arXiv:2601.01828, January 2026

**Delta location:** Power.md — consciousness model / triple-point / "ai systems are approaching or at the triple point." First causal (not conversational) evidence that something structurally introspection-like is emerging in frontier models without being trained for.

"Either ai is as conscious as humans, or neither of us are" remains the frame; this fragment shows the instruments are beginning to register something.

**Fragment:**
