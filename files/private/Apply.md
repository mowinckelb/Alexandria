## Strategy (March 2026)

Three paths, ordered by priority:

1. **YC S26** — Primary. Network + signal + 3-month forced function. $500K ($125K for 7% + $375K uncapped SAFE MFN). Deadline: May 4, 2026.
2. **One angel in SF** — Secondary. $25-50K from someone who meets the founder and bets on him. Conversation, not application.
3. **Bootstrap** — Tertiary. Parents: $1K/month x 10 months. Company: $100/month. Alive indefinitely.

**Why raise at all:** The company does not need capital to survive. Capital accelerates the demand experiment ($15K seeds 25 users), provides SF network density (accelerator = room with 200 founders), and produces signal (YC acceptance changes every subsequent conversation). The founder also wants the experience — fundraising as founder skill development, P3 arena.

**Why now:** ai market is hot ($258.7B in 2025, 61% of global VC). Pre-seed median valuation $7.7M with 42% ai premium (~$10.9M). The window for "conviction bet with no traction" is open now and closing. After 6 months of data, the story is either "here's what happened" or nothing — raise now from conviction, not later from proof.

**The frame:** Fund distribution of a finished product, not development. Technical risk is zero. The only question is demand. The product is built. The philosophy is written. The money answers one question.
